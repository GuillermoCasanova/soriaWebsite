Business Valuations, Mergers & Acquisitions

Since markets and the economy are in constant flux, Business Valuations are no longer an option but a necessity. Business valuations is a set of procedures geared towards estimating the economic value of a business, part of a business or a specific asset. A business valuation report, completed by an accredited or certified valuation expert who is a member of a professional organization and is bound by its policies, procedures and professional standards, may be appropriate for use in courts, for financing and for tax purposes, as needed.

We have performed valuations for:

  • Purchase/sale transactions
  • Mergers/acquisitions
  • Tax reorganizations
  • Impairment valuations
  • Dissenting shareholder disputes
  • Liquidation of estates
  • Marital dissolutions
  • Purchase price allocations
  • Value assessment for litigation purposes
