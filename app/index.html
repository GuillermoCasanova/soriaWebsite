We are Soria LLC, a consulting organization integrating academic background and experience in law, finance, and accounting to find feasible solutions for our clients.

Attorneys & Accountants

with a different point of view and excellent credentials in:

  • Business Valuations, Mergers & Acquisitions

    Since markets and the economy are in constant flux, Business Valuations are no longer an option but a necessity. Business valuations is a set of procedures geared towards estimating the economic value of a business, part of a business or a specific asset.

  • Litigation Support

    We can offer expert testimony on your behalf in legal cases involving financial matters in dispute such as lost profits and economic damages, business valuation, tracing of hidden assets, forensic accounting, shareholder disputes, and bankruptcy/insolvency among others.

  • Forensic Accounting-Fraud Services

    We can help your company or attorneys detect, deter, and investigate fraud. In detection and investigation services we can include asset tracing, forensic accounting, computer forensics, interviews and interrogations, and expert reports and testimony.

  • ERM–Risk Management Services

    We can help with risk management and risk reduction through an Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) evaluation or a customized design of accounting and auditing systems and procedures.

  • Due Diligence Services

    Due Diligence is a field of specialized knowledge, which helps reduce the risk of acquiring unseen liabilities and risk exposures associated with a commercial transaction. As a function of due diligence and investment analysis, we will advise on a wide variety of financial transactions.

  • Transfer Pricing Studies

    We perform transfer pricing studies according to IRC section 482 and the OECD standards. Transfer prices are the prices at which services, tangible property and intangible property are traded across international borders between related parties.

  • Internal Audits

    We have significant experience in internal audit outsource and specifically the following functions: Preparation of Audit Charter, Preparation of Annual Audit Plan, preparation of work programs, and assistance in the implementation or execution of the audit plan.
