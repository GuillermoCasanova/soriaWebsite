ERM–Risk Management Services

Soria, LLC, may help with risk management and risk reduction through an Enterprise Risk Management (ERM) evaluation or a customized design of accounting and auditing systems and procedures.

ERM are methods and processes used by organizations to manage risks and identify opportunities related to the achievement of their goals and objectives. ERM provides a framework for risk management, which typically involves identifying particular events or circumstances relevant to the organization's objectives (risks and opportunities), assessing them in terms of likelihood and magnitude of impact, determining a response strategy, and monitoring progress. By identifying and proactively addressing risks and opportunities, business enterprises protect and create value for their stakeholders, including owners, employees, customers, regulators, and financing institutions. Inadequate risk management can result in severe consequences for companies as well as individuals.

An ERM usually includes the evaluation of these four main risk areas:

  • Strategic
  • Compliance
  • Financial
  • Operational

We have performed the following projects regarding ERM:

  • ERM awareness training
  • ERM program implementation
  • ERM program effectiveness monitoring
  • Evaluation of compliance with laws and regulations
  • Evaluation of effectiveness and efficiency of operations
  • Assessment of internal controls
