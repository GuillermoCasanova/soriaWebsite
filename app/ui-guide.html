Typography

Typography for Soria, llc. Uses 'Asar' for headings and 'Roboto Condensed' for all other body copy. We follow a 24px base line-height for achieving vertical rhythm throughout the site. All line-heights, margins, and paddings start at 12 and from there are multiples of 24
